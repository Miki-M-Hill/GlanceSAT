## Introduction

Welcome to **Glance: SAT® Vocab Prep** (“**Glance**,” “**we**,” “**us**,” or “**our**”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our iOS mobile application (the “**App**”).

**Summary:** Glance is designed to store your learning data **on your device**. We do **not** require an account, and we do **not** operate servers that receive your vocabulary progress, quiz answers, or onboarding answers. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

By downloading or using Glance, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect and Store

### 1. Information you provide in the App (stored locally)

During onboarding, in Settings, and through normal use, the App may store the following **on your iPhone or iPad**:

| Category | Examples | Purpose |
|----------|----------|---------|
| **Onboarding & goals** | Whether you have taken the SAT before; prior Reading/Writing score range (if provided); target Reading/Writing score goal; quick baseline quiz answers; optional “widget installed” flag | Personalize your experience and plan recap |
| **Learning preferences** | Daily quiz reminder time; optional SAT test date you choose in Settings | Schedule reminders and show your goal date |
| **Widget preferences** | Home/Lock Screen widget style, color theme, and typography choices from Widget Studio | Display widgets the way you configure them |
| **Quiz & vocabulary progress** | Words encountered, mastery/familiarity levels, quiz sessions, correct/incorrect answers, spaced-repetition scheduling, category performance, streak-related state | Power daily quizzes, Insights, and adaptive review |
| **Optional preview access** | Whether you started a limited-time full-access preview (if offered in the App) | Provide temporary access before a paid subscription |

This information is saved using **on-device storage** (including Apple’s SwiftData and UserDefaults) and, where needed for widgets, in an **App Group** container shared only between the Glance app and Glance’s iOS widgets on **the same device**.

We do **not** transmit this learning data to Glance-operated cloud servers as part of normal App operation.

### 2. Lock Screen and Home Screen widgets

If you add a Glance widget, vocabulary content you configure may appear on your **Lock Screen** or **Home Screen**. Anyone who can see your device may see that content. Widget interactions (such as revealing a memory hook or answering a widget quiz) update your on-device learning data after you open the App or when the App refreshes in the background.

### 3. Notifications (optional)

If you allow notifications in iOS Settings, the App can schedule **local** reminders (for example, a daily evening check-in). We store your chosen reminder time on your device. We do **not** need your email or phone number to send these reminders. You can turn notifications off at any time in **Settings → Notifications → Glance**.

### 4. Purchases and subscriptions (Apple)

All purchases, free trials, renewals, billing, and refunds are handled by **Apple** through your **Apple ID** and the App Store. **We do not collect or store your payment card or bank details.**

Apple may provide us with limited subscription-related information (such as whether a subscription is active) so we can unlock premium features. That processing is subject to [Apple’s privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

### 5. When you contact us

If you email **support@glanceprep.com**, we receive the information you choose to send (such as your email address and message content) so we can respond.

### 6. Links to other services

The App may open:

- Our website (**glanceprep.com**) for Help, this Privacy Policy, and Terms of Use (in-app browser)
- **Instagram** or **TikTok** if you tap those links in Settings
- **Apple** subscription management pages if you manage a subscription

Those services have their own privacy policies. We do not control them.

## Information We Do Not Collect

Unless described above (such as a support email you send us), Glance does **not**:

- Require you to create a Glance account
- Sell your personal information
- Use third-party advertising networks in the App
- Use third-party analytics SDKs (such as Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar) in the current version of the App
- Upload your vocabulary lists, quiz history, or onboarding answers to Glance-operated servers for sync or backup

**Crash and performance data:** We do not currently integrate separate third-party crash-reporting SDKs. Apple may collect diagnostic information from your device according to your iOS settings and Apple’s policies; we may use that information only if Apple makes it available to us as the developer (for example, via Xcode crash logs).

If we add analytics or crash-reporting tools in the future, we will update this policy and, where required, the App Store privacy label before the update ships.

## Data Retention

- **On-device learning data** is kept while you use the App so your progress continues across sessions.
- If you **delete the App**, on-device data stored in the App’s container is removed as part of iOS app deletion. Data in the App Group used by widgets on the same device is also removed when the App and its associated data are deleted, subject to iOS behavior.
- **Support emails** are kept only as long as needed to respond and maintain reasonable business records.

We do not retain copies of your full learning history on Glance servers because we do not collect it there.

## Your Choices and Rights

You can:

- **Turn off notifications** in iOS Settings at any time
- **Remove widgets** from your Lock Screen or Home Screen at any time
- **Change or clear preferences** inside the App (reminder time, widget style, SAT date, etc.)
- **Manage or cancel subscriptions** through your Apple ID → Subscriptions
- **Delete the App** to remove locally stored learning data from your device
- **Contact us** at **support@glanceprep.com** with privacy questions or requests

Because we do not operate accounts or a central database of your learning progress, we generally **cannot** restore your progress if you delete the App or replace your device unless you use an Apple device backup that includes app data.
