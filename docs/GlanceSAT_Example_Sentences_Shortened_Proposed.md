- **Proposed (14 words):** Though not the strongest Thundercats, wily Kit and Kat were the most clever tricksters.

## Winsome

- **Original (17 words):** After such a long, frustrating day, I was grateful for Chris’s winsome attitude and childish naivete.
- **Proposed (12 words):** After a frustrating day, I welcomed Chris's winsome attitude and childish naivete.

## Wrath

- **Original (19 words):** Did you really want to incur her wrath when she is known for inflicting the worst punishments legally possible?

## Zealous

## Zenith

- **Proposed (14 words):** I never told Nelly that her one hit marked the zenith of her career.

## Zephyr

- **Original (18 words):** If not for the zephyrs that were blowing and cooling us, our room would’ve been unbearably hot.
- **Proposed (12 words):** Without the zephyrs cooling us, our room would have been unbearably hot.
